# Orders and Shipping Guide

## How to Place an Order

### Step 1: Product Selection
- Browse our catalog at www.healthcaresense.com/catalog
- Contact your sales representative for personalized recommendations
- Request a quote via email: sales@healthcaresense.com
- Phone orders: 1-800-MED-SALE (Monday-Friday, 8 AM - 6 PM EST)

### Step 2: Quote and Approval
Once you select equipment:
- We provide detailed quotation within 24 hours
- Quote includes: equipment cost, delivery, installation, training
- Quote validity: 30 days from issue date
- Financing options available (see section below)

### Step 3: Purchase Order
Submit your purchase order to:
- Email: orders@healthcaresense.com
- Fax: 1-800-FAX-ORDER
- Online portal: portal.healthcaresense.com

Required information:
- Company name and billing address
- Shipping address (if different from billing)
- Contact person and phone number
- Payment terms
- Special delivery instructions

### Step 4: Order Confirmation
Within 24 hours of receiving your PO:
- Order confirmation email sent
- Unique order tracking number assigned (format: ORD-XXXXX)
- Estimated delivery date provided
- Installation schedule (if applicable)

## Order Tracking

Track your order anytime:
- Online: www.healthcaresense.com/track
- Enter your order number (ORD-XXXXX)
- Phone: 1-800-TRACK-IT
- Email updates: Automatic at each shipment milestone

Order statuses:
- **Pending**: Order received, awaiting processing
- **Confirmed**: Order verified, preparing for shipment
- **In Production**: Custom orders being manufactured
- **Ready to Ship**: Packed and ready for pickup by carrier
- **Shipped**: En route to your facility
- **Out for Delivery**: Local delivery scheduled
- **Delivered**: Received at your facility
- **Installed**: Equipment installed and commissioned

## Shipping Options

### Domestic Shipping (USA)

**Standard Ground:**
- Transit time: 5-7 business days
- Cost: Included for orders over $5,000
- Best for: Non-urgent equipment, accessories

**Expedited Shipping:**
- Transit time: 2-3 business days
- Cost: $200-$500 depending on size/weight
- Best for: Spare parts, urgent replacements

**Next-Day Air:**
- Transit time: 1 business day
- Cost: $500-$1,500
- Best for: Critical spare parts, emergency replacements

**White Glove Delivery:**
- Includes: Room-of-choice delivery, unpacking, debris removal
- Transit time: Scheduled based on your availability
- Cost: $300-$800
- Best for: All major equipment
- Required for: MRI, CT, and other large imaging systems

### International Shipping

We ship to over 100 countries worldwide:
- Air freight: 5-10 business days
- Ocean freight: 4-8 weeks
- All necessary export documentation handled
- Customs clearance assistance provided
- DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) available

International shipping costs vary by:
- Destination country
- Equipment size and weight
- Shipping method
- Customs and duties

## Returns and Exchanges

### Return Policy
- 30-day return window from delivery date
- Equipment must be in original condition
- Restocking fee: 15% of purchase price
- Return shipping at customer's expense
- Custom or configured items are not returnable

### Exchange Policy
- Exchanges allowed within 30 days
- Must be for equal or greater value
- Size/weight differences may incur additional shipping charges

### Damaged or Defective Items
If equipment arrives damaged or defective:
1. Document damage with photos immediately
2. Note on delivery receipt before signing
3. Contact us within 24 hours: claims@healthcaresense.com
4. Keep all packaging materials
5. We will arrange:
   - Inspection by our technician
   - Repair if minor damage
   - Replacement if major damage or defect
   - Full refund if equipment cannot be repaired

No restocking fee or return shipping charges for damaged/defective items.

## Tips for Smooth Delivery

1. **Site Preparation:**
   - Ensure loading dock access for large equipment
   - Verify doorway widths and ceiling heights
   - Have electrical and plumbing ready (if required)
   - Clear pathways to installation room

2. **Be Ready for Delivery:**
   - Have designated contact person available
   - Arrange for staff to receive shipment
   - Inspect equipment before accepting delivery
   - Have PO number ready for driver

3. **Installation Prep:**
   - Schedule installation 1-2 weeks after delivery
   - Arrange for IT/biomedical staff availability
   - Plan for operator training time
   - Ensure power/HVAC requirements met

4. **Documentation:**
   - Keep all packing lists and paperwork
   - Document serial numbers immediately
   - File warranty registration
   - Keep installation certificates

For any questions about ordering or shipping, our team is here to help make your experience smooth and hassle-free.
