# Warranty and AMC Policy Guide

## Standard Warranty Terms

### Coverage Period

All healthcare equipment purchased from HealthcareSense includes a manufacturer's warranty:

**Diagnostic Imaging Equipment:**
- Ultrasound Systems: 24 months comprehensive warranty
- X-Ray Systems: 36 months parts warranty, 12 months labor
- CT Scanners: 36 months comprehensive warranty
- MRI Scanners: 60 months parts warranty, 24 months labor

**Laboratory Equipment:**
- Analyzers and Automated Systems: 24 months
- Centrifuges and Microscopes: 18 months
- Incubators and Sterilizers: 12 months

### What's Covered

The warranty covers:
- Defects in materials and workmanship
- Manufacturing defects
- Parts replacement at no additional cost
- Labor for repairs at our authorized service centers
- Firmware and software updates
- Annual calibration (first year only)

### What's Not Covered

The following are excluded from warranty coverage:
- Damage due to improper use or negligence
- Unauthorized modifications or repairs
- Consumables and accessories (probes, tubes, detectors)
- Cosmetic damage that doesn't affect functionality
- Damage from power surges or improper electrical supply
- Normal wear and tear
- Acts of nature (floods, lightning, earthquakes)

### Warranty Claim Process

To file a warranty claim:
1. Contact our support team at support@healthcaresense.com or call 1-800-MEDEQUIP
2. Provide equipment serial number and purchase documentation
3. Describe the issue and any error messages
4. Our technician will diagnose the problem remotely if possible
5. If on-site service is needed, schedule will be arranged within 48 hours
6. Parts will be shipped overnight if replacement is required
7. All covered repairs will be completed at no charge

## Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)

### Why Choose AMC?

An AMC provides peace of mind and ensures your equipment operates at peak performance:
- Prevents unexpected breakdowns
- Extends equipment lifespan by 40-60%
- Reduces downtime to less than 2% annually
- Maintains warranty compliance
- Ensures regulatory compliance
- Priority service response

### AMC Tiers

**Tier 1 - Basic AMC**
- Quarterly preventive maintenance (PM) visits
- Cleaning and calibration
- Visual inspection and safety checks
- PM parts included (filters, belts, etc.)
- Standard response time: 72 hours
- Coverage hours: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM
- Annual cost: 8-10% of equipment value

**Tier 2 - Standard AMC**
Everything in Tier 1, plus:
- Emergency breakdown coverage
- All repair parts included (excluding high-value items)
- Priority response time: 48 hours
- Coverage hours: Monday-Saturday, 8 AM - 6 PM
- Remote diagnostic support
- Firmware and software updates
- Annual cost: 12-15% of equipment value

**Tier 3 - Premium AMC**
Everything in Tier 2, plus:
- 24/7 emergency response
- Guaranteed response time: 24 hours
- All parts included (no exclusions)
- Loaner equipment during extended repairs
- Quarterly performance reports
- Annual comprehensive system upgrade
- Operator retraining as needed
- Dedicated account manager
- Annual cost: 18-22% of equipment value

### AMC Enrollment

Eligibility:
- Equipment must be within 5 years of manufacture date
- Must pass initial inspection (free for equipment purchased from us)
- Enrollment must occur before warranty expiration or within 30 days after

How to enroll:
1. Request AMC quote from your sales representative
2. Schedule initial inspection (if equipment not purchased from us)
3. Review and sign AMC agreement
4. Payment options: Annual lump sum or quarterly installments
5. Service begins immediately upon payment

### Preventive Maintenance Schedule

**Ultrasound Systems:**
- Quarterly: Probe testing, image quality verification, filter cleaning
- Semi-annually: Full system calibration, software updates
- Annually: Comprehensive safety testing, electrical safety checks

**X-Ray Systems:**
- Quarterly: Tube and generator inspection, collimator calibration
- Semi-annually: Detector calibration, image quality testing
- Annually: Full electrical safety test, radiation output measurement

**MRI Scanners:**
- Quarterly: Gradient calibration, RF system checks, cryogen levels
- Semi-annually: Magnet stability testing, shim calibration
- Annually: Comprehensive safety audit, image quality phantom testing

**CT Scanners:**
- Quarterly: Detector calibration, x-ray tube assessment
- Semi-annually: Gantry alignment, image quality verification
- Annually: Full system calibration, radiation dose calibration

### Response Times and SLAs

Service Level Agreements vary by AMC tier:

**Critical Equipment (Life-support, OR equipment):**
- Tier 1: 48 hours
- Tier 2: 24 hours
- Tier 3: 12 hours

**High Priority (Diagnostic imaging, laboratory):**
- Tier 1: 72 hours
- Tier 2: 48 hours
- Tier 3: 24 hours

**Standard Priority (Office equipment, accessories):**
- Tier 1: 5 business days
- Tier 2: 72 hours
- Tier 3: 48 hours

### Geographic Coverage

AMC service is available nationwide:
- Major metropolitan areas: Next-day service available
- Regional centers: 24-48 hour service
- Remote areas: 48-72 hour service
- Expedited service available for additional fee

### Renewal and Cancellation

**Automatic Renewal:**
- AMC contracts auto-renew annually unless cancelled 60 days prior
- Renewal pricing may adjust based on equipment age and usage
- Loyalty discounts available for multi-year renewals

**Cancellation Policy:**
- 60-day written notice required
- Pro-rata refund for unused portion if cancelling mid-term
- No refund if equipment fails during AMC period

### AMC Value Proposition

Average savings with AMC vs. pay-per-service:
- Emergency repairs: $2,500 - $15,000 per incident
- Preventive maintenance visit: $800 - $2,000 per visit
- Parts markup without AMC: 40-60%
- Typical AMC ROI: Pays for itself after 1-2 service calls

## Extended Warranty Options

For equipment approaching end of standard warranty:

**Extended Warranty - 1 Year:**
- Same coverage as original warranty
- Cost: 5-8% of current equipment value
- Must be purchased before warranty expires

**Extended Warranty - 2 Years:**
- Comprehensive coverage
- Cost: 9-14% of current equipment value
- Discounted rate vs. two separate 1-year extensions

**Extended Warranty - 3 Years:**
- Maximum coverage period
- Cost: 12-18% of current equipment value
- Best value for long-term protection

## Parts Warranty

Replacement parts installed under warranty or AMC:
- New OEM parts: 90 days warranty
- Refurbished parts: 60 days warranty
- Labor for part installation: 30 days warranty
