# Healthcare Equipment Guide

## Ultrasound Machines

Ultrasound machines use high-frequency sound waves to create images of internal body structures. Our ultrasound equipment includes:
- Portable ultrasound devices for bedside imaging
- High-resolution color Doppler ultrasound systems
- 3D/4D ultrasound imaging capabilities

Typical specifications:
- Frequency range: 2-18 MHz
- Display: 15-21 inch high-resolution LCD
- Connectivity: DICOM compatible, USB, Ethernet
- Power requirements: 110-240V AC, 50/60 Hz

## X-Ray Equipment

X-ray devices produce electromagnetic radiation to create images of bones and organs. We offer:
- Digital radiography (DR) systems
- Computed radiography (CR) systems
- Mobile X-ray units for ICU and emergency departments

Technical specifications:
- Generator power: 20-80 kW
- Tube voltage: 40-150 kV
- mA range: 10-800 mA
- Image resolution: Up to 3000 x 3000 pixels

## MRI Scanners

Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) scanners use powerful magnets and radio waves to create detailed images. Our MRI systems include:
- 1.5 Tesla field strength scanners
- 3.0 Tesla high-field scanners for advanced imaging
- Open MRI systems for claustrophobic patients

Key features:
- Gradient strength: 30-45 mT/m
- Slew rate: 150-200 T/m/s
- Bore diameter: 60-70 cm
- Typical scan time: 15-90 minutes depending on sequence
